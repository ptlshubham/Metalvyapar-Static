Who we are

MetalVyapar is India's first B2B e-commerce platform for metal scrap. We offer a one stop solution for buyers and sellers to trade in variety of ferrous scrap.

While the industry has grown rapidly over the past decade, there has been limited digitalization. The high volatility in prices and ever-changing demand/supply dynamics have exerted tremendous pressure on all players.

Our mission

Easy and quick trade

Provide easy and quick scrap trade solutions in order to bring buyers and sellers closer.

Ethical and transparent practices

Prioritize transparency in everything we do. Together, we aim to build businesses that you can trust and grow with.

Build reliable network

Onboard trusted partners with rich industry experience. Ensure comprehensive service capabilities for all partners.
