How does it work

Step wise guide on how digital trades will be done on the platform.

  • Getting started: Sign up to register yourself as a buyer or seller and select the material qualities to trade in.
  • Onboarding: Our team will get in touch and help you get onboarded – registration, best practices, platform demo, etc.
  • Trade kick-off: Buyers initiate trade by sharing their demand proposal - material requirements, price, payment terms etc.
  • Trade execution: Sellers respond to buyer proposals basis availability – material type, quantity, delivery terms, etc.
  • Order completion: For all confirmed orders, buyer will pay platform fees of Rs.118/ton (inclusive of GST). Both parties can view real time updates on delivery, payment status etc.
